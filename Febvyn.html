the Febvyn

An open-world RPG and diverse gameplay design which ended up with just getting experience.

Febvyn was my one of the early project. I had experience with the previous games but they were all small scale projects. Months past, i learned pretty much things about game developing, we realized developing this project is impossible in our states. We left the project and deleted all files -because nearly 50 GB sized- and the leftover proof-of-works are there below.

What i learned and work on during developing Febvyn: * I understood how the Landscape Tool&Landscape Mesh and advanced blend on Landscape shader works.

* I understood the every basics of texturing&shaders. All texture maps and works in theory, tiling-noise, how to create both realistic and stylized textures and shaders. Using Blender and Quixel Mixer.

* How the Spline, Landmass Plug-in, Water Plug-in works.

* Lighting basics, Post-Proccess, Sky effects, FX, particles and niagara basics and the huge impact on performance they do like foliages that made with 3D high poly mesh.

* Designing levels like playing the game as a player's eyes.

* Pretty much others that i don't even realize right now.
