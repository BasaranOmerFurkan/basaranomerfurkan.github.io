I am really eager and ambitious to participate in new projects to improve myself and to see what i'm capable of. Here are some of the projects that i worked on as a Game Designer, Level Designer, 2D&3D Game Artist.

Chef the Runner

Chef the Runner was the our very first game me and my friends developed at Boğaziçi GameJam which I took part with as a Game Designer,Level Designer, Concept Level Artist roles. Mevlüt Dinç has appreciated the Game's old-school designed integrity. Chef the Runner was a runner game which you could collect cook materials that goes to the inventory, at the end of every level, you combinate those cook materials into one meal, you could arrange your inventory as you like. Made with Unity Game Engine, developed in 48 Hours, with 2 Game Developers and me.

Tweak the Weak

Tweak the Weak was the our second game made at Global GameJam which i took part as a Game Designer, Level Designer, Concept Level Artist, Game Artist, Cinematic Content Creator and Vocalization. Right after i experienced game developing with Unity 3D, i've realized there is also another game engine, Unreal Engine 4. I immediately started to learn it's interface and workflows etc. Unreal Engine 4 made me look the whole perspective of game developing and carried me another level and ambition later on. Tweak the Weak is a 'Whoever Cheats is the Winner' themed-game which we helping with cheating the weakest gladiator to earn his freedom, there was also gambling mechanic for another fights we cannot impact. Made with Unreal Engine 4, developed in 48 Hours, with 2 Game Developers and Me.

Febvyn

After our development experience and self-development on Unreal Engine 4 with tutorials etc. I thought we are ready for our dream Open-World RPG with combat and diverse gameplay project. I lead my two Game Developer friends -which we worked many times before- into this project. I was fascinated about what Unreal Engine 4 capable of, what can we achive with the Epic Store Assets that we have. We thought we could easily merge and integrate assets into this AAA-but-indie project. Febvyn is the game that player roams across the islands which has each different story, each different gameplay and each different whole graphic style. I took part in that project as a Game Designer, Level Designer, Concept Level Artist, Content Writer, 2D&3D Game Artist, Optimization and Cinematic Creator. Was going to developed with Unreal Engine 4, with-in 1-year, with 2 Developers and me.

Mad Scientist

Mad Scientist is a game I designed and developed for my university study. It has graffiti-gun mechanic, player picks from chemicals for each tasks on the levels. Basically for Level 1: player picks O(Oxygen) and two H(Hydrogen) to create H2O. I was responsible for every aspect of developing, using 3D Mesh assets. Developed with Unreal Engine 4 by me.
